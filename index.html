About

I've enjoyed exploring programming puzzles since I started learning C and C++ in high school. While learning these low-level languages was exciting, writing Bash and Perl to automate my home network really hooked me on programming. While writing software for bottled-water delivery, I began writing apps for the web, and have never looked back.

Outside of work, I still find myself drawn to development. I try to learn a new language or framework every year. The exposure to different approaches has helped me immensely in work and personal applications.

When not at the computer, I'm a father, motorcyclist, and bicyclist. Living in Flagstaff, AZ, there is no-end to outdoor adventures to share with my family.
